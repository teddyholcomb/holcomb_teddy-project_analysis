Project Analysis

  • Project:Redesign of Website
  • Designer:Teddy Holcomb
  • Client:Spring Lake Elementary School, Altamonte Springs, FL

Site Intent

Goals for Redesign

The intent for this project is a full redesign of Spring Lake Elementary School’s website. The main priority in this redesign is to help cleanup the look, feel, and navigation of their website. By giving the code some long awaited attention and thinning out their navigation the site will be able to take it’s first steps into having the look and feel of a 21st century website. With this newly added organization, Spring Lake’s website won’t turn people away from using it, but instead make it an informational outlet that anyone will be able to navigate through for important information.

Additional Touch-Ups

Another project will be to touch-up the initial look of the website with a facelift. A change that won’t give the user the feeling that they’ve went back in time visiting the site. The school’s logo will also need some touching up, but not a total re-working. Ultimately the goal is to make this website very user friendly and keep the user involved on all the new info and events happening at Spring Lake Elementary.

Competitive Analysis

It seems that Spring Lake’s only real competition are the other elementary schools in their district. There are quite a few elementary schools for a user to choose from in the districts catalog of schools. After looking at a few of the other school’s websites it’s clear to see that with the redesign of Spring Lake’s website, they will stand out from the rest.

Demographics

Based on studies through the internet, females ranging from age 35-44 visit k12.fl.us most often. They have children and have also received some college education. The primary browsing spot for Spring Lake’s users is from their home and their average household income is anywhere from $60K - $100K yearly.

Keywords

You must use web services like Alexa and Google Adwords in order to find any keywords for the current site, as there are no visible keywords in the meta tags in the sites markup. Some of the keywords found through these services are spring lake elementary, seminole county schools, spring lake, elementary school.

Site Statistics

  • At least 34.63% of users get to the site from google.com and 4.71% or less through other sites.
  • Average load time for the housing site k12.fl.us is 0.958 seconds. 75% of other sites are slower.
  • Average time spent on the site is anywhere from 4 – 5 minutes.

Target Audience

According to research through analysis systems, I’ve come to the conclusion that the target audience for the Spring Lake site is primarily residential. The specific user researching information about what school they would like to enroll their child appears to be mothers. The ethnicity of the users is primarily Caucasian and African American.

The mother that has been identified as the main user through research has basic web navigational skills, so the site needs to be laid out in such a way that she can easily navigate through the site and find anything she is looking for. This could be a typical 9 to 5 working mother and quite possibly raising her child/children on her own.
